Welcome to Bike Feed

Bike feed is short for bike feedback. We want to encourage citizen / public participation around biking in your community. This means we want you to participate in identifing problems with the current bike network and infrastructure. Allow you to help make suggestions as to where new bike routes could be planned or are needed. Give you the power to identify where problems occur and email the correct Public Official about this problem.
